Arizona legislation matches other states'

The Arizona Center for Investigative Reporting compared the text of every bill introduced during the 52nd Legislature's second regular session to more than 500,000 bills from other states using a machine learning algorithm designed by data scientists at the University of Chicago. The algorithm assigned scores to phrases in Arizona bills by how closely they matched other states' legislation.
